Selected Projects

Abrasive Storage Hopper

BWS Manufacturing Ltd.

Used Autodesk Inventor to design an abrasive storage hopper for integration with existing blast equipment. The design emphasized cost-efficiency through standard stock profiles while ensuring efficient flow and easy maintenance.

  • CAD work included sheet metal plate sizing for manufacturability, wire mesh sizing and material selection.
  • Designed HSS forklift interfaces for safe and efficient maintenance removal.
  • Performed simple static load calculations to verify structural safety factors.
Autodesk Inventor Structural Analysis DFM

Vehicle Dynamic Modelling

UNB Formula SAE

Developed a vehicle simulation using MATLAB to optimize suspension geometry. The model analyzed the vehicle's transient response to road inputs to inform spring and damper selection.

  • Built a Simulink model to simulate time-domain response to road inputs under different damper tunings.
  • Wrote MATLAB scripts to compute wheel loads under combined lateral/longitudinal acceleration. The analysis included ride frequencies, roll gradient, and detailed load breakdowns to support an iterative selection of wheel bearings, springs, and motion ratios.
  • Made a sketch to define coordinates and derive the coupled differential equations governing sprung and un-sprung base excitation response.
